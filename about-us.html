Bekaefe is a family-owned company that specializes in crafting high-quality and timeless furniture. Our legacy is rooted in the iconic Butterfly chair, which first took flight in 1938 when Argentine designers Bonet, Kurchan, and Ferrari Hardoy created a design classic that quickly became a symbol of modernity and elegance. At Bekaefe, we are dedicated to preserving the history and quality of this timeless piece.

We understand that furniture is more than just a functional item - it's a reflection of personal style and taste. That's why we carefully select premium materials, such as locally sourced leather, to create chairs that are not only beautiful but also built to last. We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact and work with local craftsmen to ensure that our manufacturing process is sustainable.

Each chair we produce is a statement of our love for craftsmanship, design, and the rich cultural heritage of Argentina. Our dedication to quality and attention to detail is evident in every piece we create. We are proud to offer our customers a unique and timeless piece of furniture that represents the elegance and modernity of the iconic Butterfly chair.
