Materials

Our Butterfly chairs are crafted using premium Argentine cowhide leather that is machine-stitched, a technique that ensures precision and durability.

The steel frame of our chairs is made from solid 12mm or 10mm rods, depending on the model, and is available in a sleek black or chrome finish.

We work with local craftsmen who share our commitment to responsible manufacturing practices, and we use advanced machinery to ensure precision in every detail.

Sustainability

We source our leather from small-scale, family-owned farms that adhere to sustainable and ethical practices. We strive to create products that will last for generations, reducing the need for frequent replacements.
